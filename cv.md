- Outputs: Posters at National Epidemiology Conference (9th), PKU AI Tech Fest 2025; oral presentation at PKU Medical Forum 2024.

- Outputs: 1 invention patent filed; 1 manuscript in preparation.

**3. Diet and later-onset ulcerative colitis in Chinese adults**
- Content: Prospective associations of dietary factors/patterns with later-onset ulcerative colitis.
- Methods: Survival analysis, factor analysis.
- Outputs: 1 SCI paper and 1 SCI commentary published.
